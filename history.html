Company History

Leonardo Orellana – Owner/President


Leonardo was born in Santiago, Chile, where he learned traditions of family value in his father’s business. As the family business grew, Leonardo managed over 100 employees and expanded the business into state universities, hospitals, hotels and factories. In 1988 he came to the United States. His passion for design and building led him to learn all the phases of construction, gaining experience with several construction companies. In 2004, Leonardo became a state certified general contractor and with the strong support of his wife, Stella Tariche, Humberstone Construction Company was born. Today, his sons, Leonardo Jr, and John have joined Humberstone in their commitment to be an outstanding construction company.
